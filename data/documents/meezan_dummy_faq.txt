Meezan Bank Islamic Banking FAQs

Q1: Does Meezan Bank charge interest?
A1: No, Meezan Bank operates strictly on Islamic Shariah principles. All products are interest-free (Riba-free) and are based on Islamic contracts such as Murabaha, Ijarah, and Musharakah.

Q2: What is the Meezan Asaan Account?
A2: It is a basic banking account designed for individuals who want to open an account with minimal documentation and a low initial deposit.

Q3: Are Meezan Bank's policies audited?
A3: Yes, Meezan Bank has a dedicated Shariah Compliance Department and a Shariah Supervisory Board that regularly audits and approves all products and services.
